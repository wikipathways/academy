Quality Assurance Protocol

This is a quality assurance protocol for WikiPathways. We have a roster of community editors who work through this protocol on a weekly basis. Let us know if you'd like to join the roster!

Whether you are on the roster or not, feel free to step through the protocol and complete the steps. It should take less than 30 minutes.

Thanks for helping to make WikiPathways a high-quality resource for pathway information!
