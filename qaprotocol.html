Quality Assurance Protocol

This is the quality assurance protocol for WikiPathways. We have a roster of community curators who work through this protocol on a weekly basis. Let us know if you'd like to join the roster! Whether you are on the roster or not, feel free to step through the protocol and complete the steps. It should take less than 30 minutes.

Thanks for helping to make WikiPathways a high-quality resource for pathway information!

Note: The ongoing transition to the new WikiPathways site impacts the QA protocol in the following ways:

  • Pathway edits are reviewed on the classic site.
  • Only pathways tagged as "approved" are included in the new site.
  • Try to do the Update Approval tags multiple times during your curation week, to minimize the delay for new edits to appear on the new site.
