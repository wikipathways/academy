Introduction to WikiPathways

Background

To treat and prevent disease we must understand how cells work. Cellular functions are performed by a dynamic set of proteins and metabolites that form "pathways" of interactions and reactions. Scientists around the world continue to discover thousands of new pathways each year.

WikiPathways collects these pathways in a format that can be searched, updated and used by scientists, patients, students or anyone! All content is free and open access. Our approach is similar to Wikipedia: we provide easy-to-use tools to allow any individual to contribute content.

We would like you to help us add pathways to WikiPathways!

WikiPathways Academy will walk you through the process of becoming a pathway author. We start with basic terminology and rapidly progress to using the authoring tools. Soon, you will be able to create your own pathway models for your research or published work, and using these models to visualize your research, and provide valuable knowledge to other scientists, patients and students around the world.
