Biology 101 - Central Dogma

Background

The Central Dogma of Biology was first stated by Francis Crick in 1958 and explains how information is passed between genes and proteins. To understand pathways, we first need a clear understanding of the basic molecule types and how they relate to each other through transcription and translation.
