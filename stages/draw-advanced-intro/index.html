Advanced Modeling - Overview

Background

This path includes several more advanced modeling tasks, such as depicting cellular compartments, representing specific types of processes like transcription/translation and transport and representing post-translational modifications like phosphorylations.

This is the last learning path in the Academy, after these tasks you will be ready to take on any pathway modeling project! If you are interested in getting involved as an admin curator at WikiPathways, check out our Quality Assurance Protocol.
