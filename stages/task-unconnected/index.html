WikiPathways QA - Connect Interactions to Nodes

Goal

The goal of this task is to make sure all interactions in WikiPathways models properly connect to nodes.

Instructions

  • You will need to have PathVisio and the WikiPathways plugin installed and running.
  • Click the pathway image or title below to view the pathway at WikiPathways.
  • The red Fix interactions tag displayed at WikiPathways will indicate how many interactions are unconnected.
  • To open the pathway in PathVisio, search WikiPathways for the pathway title or WPID using the WikiPathways App, under Plugins > WikiPathways > Search.
  • In PathVisio, connect all unconnected interactions. Note: use ctrl-L to highlight unconnected ends.
  • If an interaction points to nothing and therefore cannot be connected, replace the interaction with a graphical line, available under Graphical elements in the Objects panel.
  • When all interactions are connected, click Plugins > WikiPathways > Update and enter a description of your edits. Remove the Fix interactions tag.
  • Click Next pathway to load the next pathway and repeat the process.
