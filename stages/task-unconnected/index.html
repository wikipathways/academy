WikiPathways: Connect Interactions to Nodes

Background

For pathway models to be maximally useful for downstream network analysis, data nodes should all be connected to interactions. The "Fix interactions" tag at WikiPathways identifies pathways with unconnected interactions. Our goal is to make sure all interactions in WikiPathways models are connected.

Instructions

  • For the pathway below, click the image or title to view/edit the pathway at WikiPathways. You will need to be logged in at WikiPathways in order to access the editing controls.
  • The red "Fix interactions" tag will indicate how many interactions are unconnected. In the pathway editor, connect all unconnected interactions.
  • If an interaction points to nothing and therefor can't be connected, replace the interaction with a graphical line, available under "Graphical elements" in the Objects panel.
  • When all interactions are connected, save the pathway.
  • Remove the "Fix interactions" tag.
  • Click "Verify" below.
  • Click "Next pathway" to load the next pathway.
