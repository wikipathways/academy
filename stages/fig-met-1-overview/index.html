Setting Pathway Information Free - Overview

Background

This quest desribes the process of curating a pathway model from existing information. The information, or content, underlying a pathway model can come from many different sources, for example published literature, your own research or other types of pathway information resources, like websites. Most commonly, the pathway information comes from one of more published articles.

To find a published paper with pathway information, here are some resources:

25 Years of Pathway Figures
The WikiPathways team has searched the literature over the past 25 years for pathway figures. This interactive tool lets you filter, search and view their findings. Search for your favorite gene, disease, or search by figure or pathway title.
