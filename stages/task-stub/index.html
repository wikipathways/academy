WikiPathways QA - Assess Stub Pathways

Goal

The goal of this task is to address stub pathways WITHIN 1 YEAR from the date it was first tagged, to avoid accumulating low-quality, incomplete content.

Instructions

  • Click the pathway image or title below to view the pathway at WikiPathways. You will need to be logged in at WikiPathways.
  • Assess whether the pathway is worth keeping and eventually completing. Is the content original? Is the bulk of the work complete in terms of datanode annotation, connections and layout?
    • If no: add the Request deletion tag.
    • If yes: Consider how to best rally support for making improvements to the pathway:
      • Open an Improve Pathway Curation Task for the pathway. The list of pathway curation tasks makes the pathway more visible to potential curators.
      • Recruit relevant domain experts to complete the pathway.
      • If you have relevant domain knowledge, complete the pathway on your own.
  • Click Next pathway to load the next pathway and repeat the process.

If you have a question related to one of the pathways you reviewed, report it to our curation issues tracker by clicking the button below. Issues reported here will be discussed in the Community Curation meetings.
