WikiPathways: Create Accounts

Background

Now things really get interesting... Are you ready to contribute to a scientific database? WikiPathways is a wiki dedicated to biological pathways. The curated and approved pathway models at WikiPathways are used in research, data visualization and education. There are thousands of pathways already modeled and released, but there are many more thousands yet to be captured. This is where YOU come in!

Create WikiPathways Account

The first step is to visit WikiPathways and create your author account. You will use this in all subsequent challenges so that your work can be acknowledged by the community.

  • Go to WikiPathways.
  • If you are interested, you can review the license and terms of use (in brief, it's all free, open source and open access).
  • Click on login/create account.
  • Solve the puzzle to prove you are not a robot.
  • Complete the form and click Create account.
  • Verify your new account below to continue!

Create GitHub Account

We are in the process of migrating to a new website, which works with GitHub. During the transition period, only a WikiPathways account is required for authoring pathways, but we will soon be requiring a GitHub account. Unless you already have a GitHub account, we recommend creating one when you create your WikiPathways account.

  • Go to GitHub and create an account.
  • Pro-tip: Your profile picture on WikiPathways.org comes directly from your associated GitHub account. Update your avatar on your GitHub settings page to update the picture shown in your WikiPathways author profile.
