WikiPathways: Create Account

Background

Now things really get interesting... Are you ready to contribute to a scientific database? WikiPathways is wiki dedicated to biological pathways. The curated and approved pathway models at WikiPathways are used in research, data visualization and education. There are thousands of pathways already modeled and released, but there are many more thousands yet to be captured. This is where YOU come in!

Challenge

The first step is to visit WikiPathways and create your author account. You will use this in all subsequent challenges so that your work can be acknowledged by the community.


  • To complete this challenge, go to www.wikipathways.org
  • If interested, you can review the license and terms of use (in brief, it's all free, open source and open access)
  • Click on login/create account
  • Solve the puzzle to prove you are not a robot
  • Complete the form and press 'Create account'
  • Respond to the email verification
  • Verify your new account below to continue!
