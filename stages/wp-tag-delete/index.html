WikiPathways: Tag for Deletion

Background

Congratulations! That covers the basics of editing and annotating pathways at WikiPathways. The next stage is working with pathway figures from actual publications. But before moving on, we need to clean up after ourselves... To do this, we will make use of Quality Tags, which help define the state or special focus of a given pathway at WikiPathways.

Challenge

Tag your pathway to mark it for deletion at WikiPathways. You will need to be logged in at WikiPathways.


  • To complete this challenge, go to your tutorial pathway at WikiPathways.
  • Log in, if you haven't already (see link in upper-left corner), and return to the page.
  • Scroll down to the Quality Tags section.
  • Click the + icon at the bottom of the section and choose Request deletion from the pulldown.
  • Enter a bit of text providing your reasoning, e.g., "this was just a test".
  • Click the green checkmark to add the tag to the pathway.
  • Done! Now let's verify your work below.
