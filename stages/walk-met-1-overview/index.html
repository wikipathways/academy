Cholesterol Biosynthesis - Overview

Background

We have all heard of cholesterol. Having too much of it is bad for the heart. But not having enough can also be risky, especially during development. Cholesterol is so important that every cell in every animal is equiped with the pathway below so they can synthesize it from byproducts of other pathways.
